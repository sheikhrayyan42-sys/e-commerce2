Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Shipping

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

We typically process and dispatch orders within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping within Pakistan takes an additional 3-5 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has been shipped.

What are your shipping charges?

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over PKR 3500 within Pakistan. A flat rate of PKR 250 applies to orders below this amount.

Can I track my order?

Yes, absolutely! Once your order has been shipped, we will send you an email with your tracking number and a link to the courier's website, where you can follow its journey.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for all unworn, unwashed items with original tags. To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team with your order number.

How do I exchange an item?

If you need to exchange an item for a different size or color, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order. We'll be happy to help you with the exchange process, subject to stock availability.

Products & Care

How should I care for my RAKH garments?

To ensure the longevity of your clothing, we recommend machine washing on a cold, gentle cycle and hanging to dry. Please refer to the care label inside each garment for specific instructions.
